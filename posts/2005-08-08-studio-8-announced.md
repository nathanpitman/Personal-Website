Macromedia have gone public on ‘“Studio 8”:http://www.macromedia.com/software/studio/’. The final installment perhaps before we see a combined effort from the [Macromedia and Adobe](https://web.archive.org/web/20100113103632/http://nathanpitman.com/journal/344/adobe-to-acquire-macromedia) software development teams.
