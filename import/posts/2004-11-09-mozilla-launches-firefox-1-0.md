*The [Mozilla Foundation](https://web.archive.org/web/20110927133049/http://www.mozilla.org/) today released Firefox 1.0, the first major new product release since June 2002. Firefox 1.0 is the completion of roughly 2 years of work on the revolutionary new browser. The release follows up the hugely successful Preview Release which had over eight million downloads.*

It doesn’t appear to be on the public pages of mozilla.org yet but the folders and files are there. [Go get it!](https://web.archive.org/web/20110927133049/http://207.200.85.49/pub/mozilla.org/firefox/releases/1.0/)
