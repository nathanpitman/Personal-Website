[Trevor McCauley](https://web.archive.org/web/20091225022939/http://www.senocular.com/) writes:

*When I was a kid I drew a lot. When I couldn’t think of anything to draw, I would close my eyes and randomly mark 12 dots on a blank piece of paper. Then I would connect the dots and examine the resulting shape to figure out what it could be – what I would attempt to draw next. This shape attempts to do the same. I called it [Inspire](https://web.archive.org/web/20091225022939/http://www.senocular.com/downloads/index.php?subaction=showfull&id=1096402677&ucat=8&kind=8) as it acts like (and was influenced by) my inspiration technique as a child. All it does is creates a random shape on your screen.*

![Inspire Fireworks AutoShape](/images/68.gif "Inspire Fireworks AutoShape")

*If you feel you’re running out of ideas, maybe [this shape](https://web.archive.org/web/20091225022939/http://www.senocular.com/downloads/index.php?subaction=showfull&id=1096402677&ucat=8&kind=8) can send some inspiration your way.*
