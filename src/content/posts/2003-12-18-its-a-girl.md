We had a bit of a [baby](https://web.archive.org/web/20100113133222/http://www.nathanpitman.com/babypitman/) related scare this morning. So after a quick call to NHS Direct we rushed over to the Maternity unit at Heatherwood Hospital in Ascot.

The staff there were fantastic and quickly determined that baby was ok. They decided that to be on the safe side they would do another scan, and we were delighted to discover that it’s a girl!

There are also signs that the ‘[Echogenic Bowel](https://web.archive.org/web/20100113133222/http://www.dor.kaiser.org/genetics/OurServices/EchogenicBowelMain.html)‘ which was detected at our 20 week scan is no longer there, good stuff. :)
