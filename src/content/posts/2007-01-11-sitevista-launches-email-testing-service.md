> See how your email newsletters look in a wide range of different email clients, and webmail systems. For every email you send to us, you receive two screen captures. One of the email as it is displayed in the software’s preview pane ([see an example](https://web.archive.org/web/20100106031632/http://www.sitevista.com/blog/media/ol2002sample-list.png)), and using our FullPage technology, showing you the entire message once it’s been opened ([see an example](https://web.archive.org/web/20100106031632/http://www.sitevista.com/blog/media/ol2002sample-fullpage.png)).

I’d always thought that the [Campaign Monitor](https://web.archive.org/web/20100106031632/http://www.campaignmonitor.com/) would do well to get cosy with [Paul](https://web.archive.org/web/20100106031632/http://www.salted.com/) and the rest of the [SiteVista](https://web.archive.org/web/20100106031632/http://www.sitevista.com/) team, an integrated ‘SiteVista / MailBuild’ solution would be a killer combo. API anyone?
