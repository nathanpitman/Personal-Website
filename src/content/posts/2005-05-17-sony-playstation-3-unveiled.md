[Sony](https://web.archive.org/web/20090924123241/http://www.sony.co.uk/) have officially unveiled the PlayStation 3. Where the Xbox 360 is ‘apparently’ based on the concept of ‘inhaling’ (WTF?) the PS3 looks more like it exhaled.

![PlayStation 3](/images/97.jpg "PlayStation 3")

My initial response to the product design late last night was one of disappointment, I don’t think the PS3 has the ‘ooh’ factor which the [Xbox 360](https://web.archive.org/web/20090924123241/http://nathanpitman.com/journal/356/) has, but then the PS2 was such a beautiful product, this is an evolution in design terms rather than the revolution that the 360 is compared to it’s predecessor.

There’s a ton of other related news over at [ign.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20090924123241/http://www.ign.com/) RE [PS3 specs](https://web.archive.org/web/20090924123241/http://ps3.ign.com/articles/614/614682p1.html), [Sony’s partnership with NVidia](https://web.archive.org/web/20090924123241/http://ps3.ign.com/articles/614/614774p1.html), [launch dates](https://web.archive.org/web/20090924123241/http://ps3.ign.com/articles/614/614641p1.html) and [backwards compatability](https://web.archive.org/web/20090924123241/http://ps3.ign.com/articles/614/614620p1.html "All the way back to PS1 baby!").
