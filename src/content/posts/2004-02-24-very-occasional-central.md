Today I was clearing out old apps, tidying up my machine when I came across something interesting.

![Central, only used occassionaly](/images/5.jpg "Central, only used occassionaly")

It makes me wonder how often anyone else ‘really’ uses [Central](https://web.archive.org/web/20100106033706/http://www.macromedia.com/software/central/), and when if ever there will ever be any killer applications for it. So far only the ‘Blog Reader’ has interested me, and to be fair, there are many better alternatives out there. What will become of Central I wonder, is it destined to become a one hit wonder, installed once, used once, never again… :?
