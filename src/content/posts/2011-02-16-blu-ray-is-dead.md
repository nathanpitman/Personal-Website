I was foolish enough to buy a Blu-ray player a few years back, how many times have I used it? Probably 3 or 4 times in the space of more than a year. It’s not that I don’t like watching movies, but the user experience with Blu-ray is nothing short of terrible. Khoi Vinh sums my thoughts up perfectly in his post [Blu-ray Blues](http://www.subtraction.com/2011/01/10/blu-ray-blues), the comments are well worth a read also – especially if you were ever an advocate of [HD-DVD](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HD_DVD).
