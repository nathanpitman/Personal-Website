Today Jo and I decided to spoil Neve a little and buy her a new toy, to be specific a ‘Rocking Snail’! Neve checked out the snail in the store and was suitably interested, so when we got back to Jo’s parents we plonked her in the seat, fastened the belt and left her to it.

![Ooooh, I have a new toy!](/images/51.jpg "Ooooh, I have a new toy!")

The seat is intended for 9 months plus, Neve is only 5 months, but she seems to be having a whole loada fun. At one point she looked like all she needed was a cowboy hat to complete the scene!

![Hold on!](/images/52.jpg "Hold on!")

One thing is for sure, even when you’ve had a really bad day, a little girlie with smiles like this can brighten life in an instant.
