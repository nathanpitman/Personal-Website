So now the [iPod](https://web.archive.org/web/20100104140419/http://www.apple.com/ipod/) plays video, I guess we all kinda saw this coming, but it makes me wonder, should apple re-brand [iTunes](https://web.archive.org/web/20100104140419/http://www.apple.com/itunes/) to be a little more descriptive? iTunes Music Store doesn’t quite work when you’re trying to tell people that they can browse and buy Music Videos and TV Shows (and films soon I’m sure). Just wondered.
