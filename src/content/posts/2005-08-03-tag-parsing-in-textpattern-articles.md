Did you know that you can nest Texpattern tags in your article body? This is super neat (and probably pretty obvious) but I never realised until now. I’ve been fiddling with a new section called ‘etc’ ([maniacal rage](https://web.archive.org/web/20100104184606/http://www.maniacalrage.net/etc/) inspired name) and wanted to add articles in the usual manner but needed to list a bunch of file downloads. At first I thought I’d have to hard code the relevant tags into my page template… Hoorah for tag parsing in articles! :)

With an [RC5](https://web.archive.org/web/20100104184606/http://forum.textpattern.com/viewtopic.php?id=9986) release over at Textpattern.com it looks like things are gathering pace and [Dean](https://web.archive.org/web/20100104184606/http://www.textism.com/) is ‘finally’ on the brink of a ’1.0’ release. I know [I’ve said it before](https://web.archive.org/web/20100104184606/http://nathanpitman.com/journal/248/textpattern-cake-nearly-ready), but I think this time they really are getting close!
