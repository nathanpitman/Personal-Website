If you work regularly with Flash you will be well aware of the annoying ‘shifting’ pixel issue. This generally occurs when you group or resize an object. More often than not the x value of the object will shift from ‘200’ for example to ’200.1’.

Now, this is easily correctable by re-entering the original value manually, but I’m sure that you would wonder why on earth the application does this in the first place. It seems that [Owen van Dijk has discovered the answer](https://web.archive.org/web/20100113141100/http://ohwhen.typepad.com/mx_traveller/2004/05/the_mystery_of_.html) courtesy of Gary Grossman…
