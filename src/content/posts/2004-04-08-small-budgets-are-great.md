I just put the finishing touches to a little budget site we’ve done here at [Mirashade](https://web.archive.org/web/20100113165328/http://www.mirashade.com/) for a [partner of ours](https://web.archive.org/web/20100113165328/http://www.effectiveps.co.uk/). This particular job reminds me how enjoyable it is to work on smaller projects where you see the job through from start to finish in a matter of days.

Working on larger jobs which span months or even years, seems to sap creative energy. It gradually becomes harder and harder to remain enthused unless something is done to reguarly inject energy into the project.

I guess what I’m saying is that ‘budget’ jobs are under rated, more often that not this is where we get to have a little more fun and be a little more expressive.
