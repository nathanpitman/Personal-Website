After hearing stories from my wife during the week about my daughters amazing new slide skills at our local soft play center (Whoosh!), I just had to witness it for myself. On Saturday morning we were there before the door was even open. We made our way to the top of the play area where the slide entrance resides and before I knew it Neve was making a run for it. Giggling already she sat herself down at the top, promptly turned herself around onto her belly and pushed off.

My video skills are a bit ropey but I had to manage sliding, aiming the camera and slowing myself down all at the same time. That plus the fact that I’m a bloke so I can’t multi-task. I think we must have ridden the slide 10 times or more. I’ll have to see if I can devise some kind of steadycam device for next time to better document the hilarity.
