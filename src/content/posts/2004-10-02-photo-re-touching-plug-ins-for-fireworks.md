Jan Esmann is an artist who has written [a collection of photo editing and restoration plug-ins for Fireworks](https://web.archive.org/web/20100106231704/http://www.powerretouche.com/Fireworks_plugins.htm). Plug-ins include:

- Black & White Studio
- Sharpness Editor
- Toned photos
- Dynamic range Compression
- Lens distortion correction
- Transparency Editor
- Contrast Editor
- Color correction
- Anti-aliasing
- Black definition
- Exposure correction
- Saturation Editor
- White-balance correction
- Radial density correction
- Noise Filter
- Edgeline drawing
- Posterizer
- Soft-filter
- Brightness Editor
