[Phireworx](http://www.phireworx.com/) release ‘Super Guides’ for Fireworks MX.

Super Guides is an axonometric projection template creator to allow you to create templates of any size, any angle and opacity for your isometric illustration needs.

Super Guides gives you control over; Projection Angle, Document Template Size and Grid Opacity. [Go check it out…](http://www.phireworx.com/)
