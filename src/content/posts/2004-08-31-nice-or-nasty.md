There’s something slightly odd about the [new iMac](http://www.apple.com/imac/design.html). Something about the design just doesn’t feel… well… balanced I guess.

![New iMac, nice or nasty?](/images/56.jpg "New iMac, nice or nasty?")

Usually when I hear that there’s a new piece of mac hardware I visit [apple.com](http://www.apple.com/) and make the appropriate ‘ooh’ and ‘aah’ noises, but this time it was more of a ‘oo…er… hmm’.

Maybe the new iMac just ‘looks’ un-balanced, maybe it’s just me… what do you think?
