Bookmark synchronisation is one of those annoying little issues. I have a PC at work, and a PC at home.., but the bookmarks are never synchronised, simply because there has been no easy way to do so.

Sure there are a few options out there that I have tried. [MyBookmarks.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20100105124130/http://www.mybookmarks.com/) is one of them, but it requires that you register with them, download an exe and have this running in your toolbar 24/7. And even then, it still seems complicated…

Well… a guy by the name of ‘Torisugari’ has developed [a great Firefox extension](https://web.archive.org/web/20100105124130/http://extensionroom.mozdev.org/more-info/booksync) which deals with Bookmark synchronisation brilliantly!

![Possibly the best Firefox Extension in the world](/images/30.jpg "Possibly the best Firefox Extension in the world")

You simply provide a FTP host, username and password and Firefox can auto upload/download the bookmarks file on startup and exit. The process is fast and efficient. Result!
