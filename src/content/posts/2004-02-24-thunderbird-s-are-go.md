Last night I made the ever so easy transition from Outlook Express to [Mozilla Thunderbird](https://web.archive.org/web/20100105192224/http://www.mozilla.org/products/thunderbird/) on my home PC.

The ‘Import’ tools took care of the transition for me, all that was left to do was set up the built in Junk/Spam mail filtering and I was away! Thunderbird is indeed go! Bye bye Microsoft.
