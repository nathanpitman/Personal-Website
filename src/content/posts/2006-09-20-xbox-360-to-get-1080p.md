**110 High-Definition Xbox 360 games showcased at TGS.**

> In addition, Microsoft announced that its fall dashboard update, scheduled for release later this year, will allow all Xbox 360 consoles around the world to output game and movie content in 1080p resolution.

Wow, didn’t see [that one](https://web.archive.org/web/20100106205051/http://www.majornelson.com/archive/2006/09/20/TGS-Press-release.aspx) coming. Up until now the PS3 was slated as the only console capable of outputting 1080p. Hats off to Microsoft for yet again demonstrating their commitment to the Xbox 360 as an evolving platform.
